Project Summary

As close to the northern hemisphere as colder the climate gets. Based on the observation of latitude, temperature, and humidity, we can observe that the increase in temperature is positively correlated to the rise in humidity. The same, as the temperature decreases, the number on humidity scale increases as well. If we take into consideration all the trends presented in this assignment, there is an indication of the positive correlation between latitude and temperature. No significant association was noted between the latitude and: humidity, cloudiness and wind speed, which are more correlated to the temperature as well.
